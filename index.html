Infrastructure & Facilities

At Globotech Talent Hub Pvt. Ltd., we provide a modern, industry-oriented training environment designed to deliver practical exposure and real-world technical competency.

Welding Training Centre

Equipped with 10 individual welding booths supporting SMAW, MIG, and TIG welding alongside modern cutting, grinding, and pipe prep stations.

Civil Mason & Steel Lab

Dedicated zones for masonry works, reinforcing steel fixing, Shuttering carpentry mockups, and real-scale structural framing.

Electrician & Plumber Lab

Industrial testing boards, live mockup power setups, circuit wiring assemblies, and domestic/commercial plumbing testing rigs.

Computer Lab & Smart Class

High-speed computer labs for AutoCAD drafting and technical documentation controller training, paired with digital classrooms.

Fire & Safety Centre

Comprehensive display of industrial safety gear, safety harness test rigs, hazard mockups, and live fire response zones.

Administrative Support

Dedicated machine shop floor, placement guidance and ticketing support cells, administrative chambers, and counseling block.

Carpentry & Machine Shop

Fully equipped woodworking and machine shop with tools for carpentry, shuttering, fabrication mockups, and precision machining exercises.

Valve & Pump Section

Live valve and pump training rigs for hands-on operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of industrial fluid control systems.

Scaffolding

Dedicated outdoor scaffolding yard with full-scale erection and dismantling practice zones meeting international CISRS and OPITO safety standards.

HVAC Training Lab

Modern HVAC training facility with functional split and ducted systems, refrigeration circuits, and airflow testing equipment for industry-ready technicians.
